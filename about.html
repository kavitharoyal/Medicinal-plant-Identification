About GreenGuide

What is GreenGuide?

GreenGuide is an innovative web application designed to identify medicinal plants using advanced deep learning techniques. It aims to bridge the gap between traditional knowledge and modern technology by providing accurate, real-time plant identification and detailed information about their medicinal uses, regional names, and preparation methods.

Why GreenGuide?

Traditional methods of medicinal plant identification are time-consuming and often require expert knowledge. GreenGuide automates this process, making it accessible to everyone, from local communities to researchers and healthcare practitioners. By leveraging deep learning, GreenGuide ensures high accuracy and efficiency in identifying medicinal plants.

Key Features

  • Accurate Identification: Uses a deep learning model based on InceptionV3 for precise plant recognition.
  • Comprehensive Database: Provides detailed information about each plant, including scientific name, regional name, medicinal uses, and preparation methods.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for easy navigation and use.
  • Regional Focus: Supports regional names (in Telugu) to cater to local communities.

Technologies Used

  • Deep Learning: TensorFlow and Keras for building and training the plant identification model.
  • Web Framework: Flask for developing the web application.
  • Database: Pandas for managing plant details stored in an Excel file.
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap for a responsive and modern user interface.

Future Enhancements

We plan to expand the dataset to include more plant species, add support for additional regional languages, and develop a mobile application for greater accessibility. Your feedback and suggestions are always welcome!
